What's an NFT?

NFT stands for "non-fungible token" - which is another way of saying it's a unique, one of a kind digital item.

You can use your Ethereum wallet (like MetaMask) to buy and store your NFT. Then, you can view your Quirky Quokka on your wallet address or in OpenSea.

Quokka?

The quokka is an extremely cute, photogenic animal that is also known as the "world's happiest animal"!

Quokkas are an endangered species that live on Rottnest Island, off the southern coast of Western Australia.

Weighing 2.5 to 5 kg and 40 to 54 cm long, quokkas are nocturnal and eat plants. Females carry their young in a pouch.

Their numbers on the Australian mainland have greatly reduced due to predators and loss of their habitat.

Quokkas earned the reputation as “the world’s happiest animals”. Since then, many photos of smiling quokkas have gone viral – and the “quokka selfie” has become a social media trend, due to their beaming smiles.
